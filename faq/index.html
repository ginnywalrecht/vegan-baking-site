Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best vegan substitute for eggs?

There are many ingredients you can use to replace eggs in your bakes and depending on what you are baking one wors better than the other but the easiest and fail-proof choice is the flax eggs.

How do I know which egg replace to use?

Eventhough I reccommend different egg replacers for different types of bakes, most egg replacers will work in different bakes, they will only create a different structure and flavour in each bake.

Can I veganize an existing recipe?

Yes! It will take a bit of research but once you know which ingredients you will need to substitute you can use this website to find a substitute for the most common non-vegan ingredients.

Can I use oil instead of butter?

Oil can be a great substitue for butter and will make your batter lighter and result in a fluffier bake, but it is not the best substitue when making biscuits or cookies as you need a type of fat that will harden after baking.
